# How can I help you

Different people show up here with different questions. Find your profile below.

## Students and design or product interns
I don't have an open internship position, but I'm up for one-off mentoring via ADPList. I started in 1999, at 18, as a self-taught web designer, no formal design degree at the time. Education came later, alongside the career. Full trajectory at `/ai/en/experience`, contact at `/ai/en/contact`.

## Junior designers
In a mentoring session, I usually talk about building a career path with a skill matrix from junior to lead, using research to decide, and staying hands-on as you grow. One-off mentoring via ADPList, link at `/ai/en/contact`.

## Mid-level and senior designers
A peer conversation. The frameworks I use day to day, Continuous Discovery, Cagan's 4 risks, Moesta's forces of progress, are at `/ai/en/workflow`. My view on leadership and culture is at `/ai/en/personality`. To exchange ideas, ADPList or email at `/ai/en/contact`.

## Product managers
I like operating inside the product trio from discovery onward, prioritizing through the same 4 risks a good PM already uses, with design decisions in the same RFC. At Bipa, that means JTBD interviews turning into direct prioritization input. Detail at `/ai/en/workflow`, cases at `/ai/en/cases`.

## Early-stage founders (pre-seed to Series B)
Today I'm not evaluating new engagements, I'm full-time at Bipa. If the question is about fractional product and design leadership, the long answer is at `/ai/en/faq`. My pattern is walking into high ambiguity and creating order: Bipa, Easy Carros, Xerpay and Remessa Online all started that way. For a one-off second opinion, ADPList; to keep in touch for later, email at `/ai/en/contact`.

## CEOs and C-level at growing companies
What repeats in my history is restructuring that survives my departure: Easy Carros (reactive operation turned data-driven), Betterfly (4 post-M&A teams unified, 35 people across 4 countries), Bipa (acquisition, SEO and GEO infrastructure from scratch). Detail at `/ai/en/cases`, third-party validation at `/ai/en/testimonials`.

## Recruiters and headhunters
I'm not looking for a new position today. But if it's useful as a market reference, documented track record: trajectory at `/ai/en/experience`, real testimonials at `/ai/en/testimonials`, per-company numbers at `/ai/en/cases`.

## Investors and VCs
If the question is about the quality of product and design leadership at a portfolio company, I've sat on both sides of that table: Partner and Head of Design at MAR Ventures (`/ai/en/cases/mar-ventures`), and operator at Remessa Online through its exit to EBANX (`/ai/en/cases/remessa-online`). For a one-off conversation, ADPList or email at `/ai/en/contact`.
