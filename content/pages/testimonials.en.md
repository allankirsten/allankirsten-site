# What people say

I document the cases with numbers. But results also show up in how the people who worked with me describe that work, not just in a metric. Below, public recommendations from my LinkedIn (`linkedin.com/in/allankirsten`), with name, role and the type of working relationship at the time. None of them is anonymous, none of them was written by me.

## Leadership

**Will Capone**, Product Marketing @ Anchorage Digital, reported directly to me at MAR Ventures and Remessa Online:
"Allan has been a true mentor. He's the real deal - besides being an excellent manager and helping people reach their full potential, Allan is also a hands-on designer with a wide range of skills. He doesn't just talk about design or delegate tasks: he actually gets work done."

**Giovanna Medeiros**, reported directly to me:
"I worked under Allan's leadership for almost a year [...] Allan is a transparent leader, easy to approach, open to hearing ideas and positions [...] From Allan I got the clearest, most illuminating feedback a leader has ever given me."

**Gabriel Dias**, reported directly to me:
"I worked for 8 months under Allan's management [...] He's the kind of manager who welcomes the team, encourages independent thinking and gives people the freedom to grow [...] He holds people accountable transparently and helps us get the best out of ourselves."

**Fabio Oliveira**, reported directly to me:
"Allan is a very easygoing person who has the precious ability to create strong connections with people. As a design leader, Allan gives the team a lot of autonomy but guarantees the strategy through frequent feedback sessions."

**Raphael Farinazzo**, CEO @ PM3, professor and angel investor, worked with me on the same team:

He dives into user experience and interaction in its details, on a very realistic basis. He is a team player with solutions that are both creative and within front-end's possibility."
