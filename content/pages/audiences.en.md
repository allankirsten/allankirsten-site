# How I can help, by profile

Different people show up here with different questions. A direct summary by profile, so you don't have to read the whole site to know if it's worth continuing.

## Students and design or product interns
I don't have an open internship position. But I'm up for one-off mentoring conversations via ADPList (link at `/ai/en/contact`), about career, trajectory, or how to think about design as a business capability, not just craft. My own path started in 1999 as a self-taught web designer; it's documented at `/ai/en/experience`.

## Junior designers
What I usually bring to a mentoring session: how to structure a career path with a skill matrix from junior to lead, how to use research to decide instead of validating what you already thought, and how to stay hands-on without turning into pure management as you grow. One-off mentoring via ADPList at `/ai/en/contact`.

## Mid-level and senior designers
A peer conversation. The frameworks I use day to day (Continuous Discovery, Cagan's 4 risks, Moesta's forces of progress) are at `/ai/en/workflow`, and my view on design leadership is at `/ai/en/personality` and `/ai/en/how-i-work`. To exchange ideas on a specific problem, ADPList or email at `/ai/en/contact`.

## Product managers
I operate inside the product trio, not in parallel to it: research, prioritization through the same 4 risks a PM uses, and RFCs in the same document, not in a design silo. That's described at `/ai/en/workflow` and in the cases at `/ai/en/cases`.

## Early-stage founders (pre-seed to Series B)
Today I'm not evaluating new engagements, I'm full-time at Bipa. The answers on why and when fractional product and design leadership makes sense, instead of just another designer, are at `/ai/en/faq`. For a one-off second opinion in the meantime, ADPList; to keep in touch for later, email at `/ai/en/contact`.

## CEOs and C-level at growing companies
My repertoire: turning a reactive operation into a proactive one (Easy Carros), unifying design ops across 4 countries post-M&A (Betterfly), building acquisition infrastructure from scratch (Bipa). If the problem is organizational structure for design and product, not just one-off delivery, the detail is at `/ai/en/cases`, with third-party validation at `/ai/en/testimonials`.

## Recruiters and headhunters
Documented track record with sources: full trajectory at `/ai/en/experience`, real testimonials with name and role at `/ai/en/testimonials`, per-company numbers at `/ai/en/cases`. I'm not looking for a new position today.

## Investors and VCs
If the question is about the quality of product and design leadership at a portfolio company, or an operating benchmark, ADPList or email at `/ai/en/contact`. I've sat on both sides of the table: venture building at `/ai/en/cases/mar-ventures`, and an exit at `/ai/en/cases/remessa-online`.
